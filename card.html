Lion

The Lion is considered the King of the Jungle...

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Weight: Up to 30 stone
  • Speed: Up to 50 mph
  • Habitat: Live together in Prides

Lions are the only cats that live in groups. They can weigh up to 30 stone and run up to 50 mph. Lions live together in Prides, the male is the protector while the female is the hunter.
